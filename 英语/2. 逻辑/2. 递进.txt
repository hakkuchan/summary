also(conj. & adv.)
	置于居中时，一般表示“也”；
	置于句首时，后跟逗号，一般表示“此外”，用于补充材料或新观点等；
	不置于句尾，在句尾表示“也”时，用 as well 或 too

moreover/furthermore/additionally (adv.)
	此外，用于补充材料或新观点等
	例句：The house is too small, and moreover/furthermore/additionally, it's too far from the office.

in addition (to)
	除……外，还……：用于补充材料或新观点等
	例句：In addition to five main nutrients, a healthy diet requires dietary fiber.

besides
	除……外，还……：用于补充材料或新观点等
	例句：
	He gave me a book, a pen and some money besides.
	Besides the two novels, I have bought two atlases.
