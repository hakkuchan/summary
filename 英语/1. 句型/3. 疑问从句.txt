3. 疑问从句
• 形式：主句 + 疑问词/if or whether + ……（陈述语序）
• 例句：
Do you know/ Could you tell you/ I wonder who will join us.
She asked me how old I was.
He wanted to know if/whether I could speak any foreign languages.
Could you tell me what time the train to Chicago leaves?
You know what's good for nerves?
I wanted to see if you could meet me for lunch tomorrow.
They just wanna see if you're okay.
We all know how expensive it can be to furnish an apartment.
