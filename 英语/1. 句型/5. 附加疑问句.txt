5. 附加疑问句

形式：陈述句(positive/negtive) + 助/系动词(negtive/positive) + 主语
附加疑问句的含义取决于音调
	a. 非疑问句，表示期望听者同意自己：It's a nice day, isn't (↓) it.
	b. 疑问句：You know where she is, don't (↑) you?

Let's 开头 + shall we
Let's go for a walk, shall we?

I am 开头 + aren't I
I'm late, aren't I.

祈使句 + will you
Open the door, will you?
Don't be late, will you?
